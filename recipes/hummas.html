Homemade Hummus

Creamy, delicious, and incredibly nutritious!

üë®‚Äçüç≥ Instructions

1

Prepare the Chickpeas

Drain and rinse the canned chickpeas thoroughly under cold water. For extra smooth hummus, you can optionally peel the chickpeas by gently rubbing them between your fingers to remove the skins (this step is optional but creates an ultra-creamy texture).

2

Blend the Tahini and Lemon

In a food processor or high-speed blender, combine the tahini and lemon juice. Blend for about 1 minute until the mixture becomes light and creamy. This step helps emulsify the tahini and creates a smoother base.

3

Add Garlic and Seasonings

Add the minced garlic, cumin, and a pinch of salt to the tahini-lemon mixture. Blend for another 30 seconds to combine all the flavors evenly.

4

Add Chickpeas and Olive Oil

Add half of the chickpeas to the food processor and blend for 1 minute. Scrape down the sides, then add the remaining chickpeas and the olive oil. Blend for another 1-2 minutes until smooth and creamy.

5

Adjust Consistency

With the processor running, slowly drizzle in 2-3 tablespoons of cold water, one tablespoon at a time, until you reach your desired consistency. The hummus should be smooth, creamy, and easily spreadable.

6

Taste and Adjust

Taste your hummus and adjust seasonings as needed. Add more salt, lemon juice, or garlic according to your preference. Blend briefly after any additions.

7

Serve and Garnish

Transfer the hummus to a serving bowl. Use the back of a spoon to create a shallow well in the center. Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil, and sprinkle with paprika, fresh parsley, or pine nuts if desired. Serve with fresh vegetables, pita bread, or crackers.
